Exit 1
The
Land Flip

THE FORMULA:

ARV = $120,000
Offer = $70,000 (58%)
FLC profit: ~$40,000
Your cut: ~$8,000

How It Works
  • Pull comparable land sales in the county (same size, last 12 months)
  • Set your ARV — what would this realistically list for on MLS?
  • Offer 55–65% of ARV — leaves room for profit, costs, and fees
  • Target: minimum $20,000 FLC profit per flip deal

Seller script: "We're cash buyers. We can close in 30 days. No commissions, no contingencies, no hassle. In exchange, we need to purchase below retail — that's how we make our business work."

Exit 2
Minor
Subdivision

EXAMPLE MATH:

8 acres · zoning: 2ac min
4 lots × $55K = $220,000
Split costs: $18,000
FLC margin: $55,000
Max offer: $147,000
Your cut: ~$29,400

How It Works
  • Confirm zoning allows splitting — what's the minimum lot size?
  • Calculate how many lots the parcel can yield
  • Research finished lot values — what are builders/buyers paying?
  • Estimate survey + plat + permit costs ($8K–$25K typically)
  • Offer = (lots × lot value) – split costs – FLC margin
  • Timeline: 3–9 months — longer than a flip, seller gets more

Seller script: "Your property has more potential than a straight sale. Because of what we can do with it, I can offer you more — but the timeline will be a bit longer. Here's why that's worth it for you..."

The System
The Exit Decision Tree

The DD determines the exit. The exit determines the offer. Follow this every time.

1

Is it off-market?

No → Retail flip only (not Allied eligible). Yes → continue.

2

20+ acres + $450K HASP + 3+ builders?

Yes → Evaluate MAJOR SUB / ALLIED path first.

3

Zoning allows 2–5 lot split?

Yes → Evaluate MINOR SUBDIVISION.

4

Strong retail land demand?

Yes → FLIP. No → Seller finance or pass.

Seller Conversations
Working the
Appointment

Every appointment has the same four goals. Master these and the rest follows.

1

Build Rapport

Be human. Be curious. People sell to people they like and trust. Don't rush to the numbers.

2

Uncover Motivation

Why are they considering selling? Estate? Taxes? Moving? Motivation drives deal structure and urgency.

3

Gather Information

Acres, zoning, access, utilities, known issues, what they think it's worth, their timeline.

4

Set the Follow-Up

"I'll do my research and have a real number for you by [specific date]." Then do your homework.

⚠️ Never commit to a number on the first call. "I'll have a real offer for you by [date]."
