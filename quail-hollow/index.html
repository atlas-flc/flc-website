Your capital sits in a preferred position — paid back alongside the development debt before any sponsors see profit distributions. This is how Chris and Nate structured it: investor protection first, upside second.

Your 13% annualized return is paid before any sponsor profit distributions. The structure treats your capital like secured debt with a guaranteed pref — not a speculative equity bet.
